Frequently asked questions

What is a Monte Carlo retirement calculator?

A Monte Carlo retirement calculator simulates thousands of possible market scenarios to estimate the probability of your retirement plan succeeding. Instead of assuming a fixed return every year, it generates random returns based on historical volatility and asset correlations â€” including crashes, booms, and stagnation. The result is a success rate: the percentage of scenarios where your money lasted the full planning horizon.

Why does RetireSIM use Student-t distributions?

Student-t distributions have heavier tails, capturing fat-tail risk more accurately.
